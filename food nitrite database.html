Introduction

The nitrite table presented here is derived from the comprehensive Nitrate, Nitrite, and Nitrosamine Database (Nitr-Navigator), utilizing global data. Each food item included in this table is sourced from more than two unique data sources (references). Therefore, it's important to note that not all foods within the database are represented here. For a complete list of included foods, please refer to the combined food table.

The Nitr-Navigator allows for the aggregation of data from multiple sources to derive a comprehensive nitrate content for specific food categories. For instance, data from various lettuce cultivars such as "Lettuce", "Lettuce, Butterhead", "Lettuce, Iceberg", "Lettuce, Loose Leaf", and "Lettuce, Romaine" can be combined to obtain a generalized nitrate content for all lettuce varieties.Please CONTACT US to discuss the data extraction and calculation procedures tailored to your specific requirements.
