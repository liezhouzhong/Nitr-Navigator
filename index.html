Our Story

We are working hard to make this database more functional and friendly.

In 2017 our team at Edith Cowan University built the first version of plant based food nitrate content, which was published in mnfr. From 2021, we started to expand the database by including animal-based foods, followed by an update of the plant based food nitrate database. The databases have supported a series of funded nitrate/nitrite-related projects, however, development and updates of the database were not funded. Still, our team has invested substaintial time and energy on them, because we believe a current and comprehensive nitrate nirite food composition database is a key infrastructure in related research...Read more
