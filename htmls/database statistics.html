Overview

The Nitrate, Nitrite, Nitrosamine Database (NNND) is the most comprehensive database for nitrate (NO3), nitrite (NO2) and nitrosamines in foods and drinks. Currently, the NNND contains over 129,000 nitrate values in more than 800 foods and drinks (except tap water but including packaged water); more than 22,000 nitrite content data in around 400 foods and drinks. The data are from over 80 countries. The nitrosamine content in foods, drinks and drugs, nitrate content in tap water will be included in the future updates.

The NNND database contains a series of data fields related to samples, sampling location (city, country, region, continent), sampling time (month, season, year), analytic methods, etc. As such, it possesses the capability to filter data and extract nitrate content based on established protocols.

Our team has developed a nitrate/nitrite calculator (as Jupyter Notebook, access through team) that can automatically and efficiently assign content values to food records while applying multiple protocols, and a NNND data portal (NitriXplorer) that can filter and visualize data.
